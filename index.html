Craft

I’m a Senior UX designer at Adobe, where I spend my days bridging creativity and technology, designing intuitive, scalable experiences with a focus on developer tools and extensibility. I’ve been doing this for nearly three decades, shaping products used by millions: from Adobe Express to platforms for Hilton Hotels, Target, the U.S. Air Force, and St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital. Lately, most of my time has been spent at the front edge of agentic user experiences. I’m figuring out what it means to design for a world where interfaces are malleable.
